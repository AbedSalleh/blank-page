About Blank Page

Blank Page is a simple, free online notepad and Markdown text editor. Sign in to write notes that automatically save and sync across your phone, tablet, and computer. Features include a distraction-free editor, dark mode, full-text search, note sharing via link, and export to Markdown or plain text.
